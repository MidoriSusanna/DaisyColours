Adult Courses

Unleash your creativity

We offer three levels of instruction: beginner, intermediate, and advanced. Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to refine your existing skills, we have a programme that suits you. Our course covers a wide range of artistic techniques, including oil and acrylic painting, drawing and watercolours. Our experienced instructors will guide you through each technique, helping you develop your style and artistic voice. At our art school, we understand that every artist is unique, and that's why we offer both individual and shared sessions. In individual sessions, you'll receive personalized attention and guidance tailored to your specific needs and goals. If you enjoy a collaborative learning environment, our shared sessions allow you to interact and learn from fellow artists. To enhance your artistic experience, we provide a mix of indoor and outdoor classes. Our indoor studio provides a comfortable and inspiring space, equipped with all the necessary materials and tools. Outdoor sessions take advantage of natural light and the beauty of nature, allowing you to draw inspiration from the world around you.

Kid Courses

Paint and play

We offer two types of kid art courses designed to inspire creativity and foster artistic growth. For children ages 3 to 6 and 6 to 10, we have specialized art classes tailored to their unique developmental and creativity needs. Through age-appropriate projects and activities, children will develop their artistic skills while expressing their imagination and individuality. In addition to our dedicated kid art courses, we also offer a special program where kids and adults can learn and create together. This unique opportunity allows children and their relatives, whether it's parents, grandparents, or siblings, to share the joy of artistic expression. It's a wonderful bonding experience where both kids and adults can explore their creativity side by side, exchanging ideas and learning from one another. Our kid and adult classes provide a supportive and inclusive atmosphere, encouraging the exploration of different art mediums and techniques.

Start dates Every week.
Time Kid course 3-6 years old: Saturday 10.00 am - 1.00 pm.
Kid course 6-10 years old: Sunday 10.00 am - 1.00 pm.
Kids and Adults course: Saturday 2.00 pm - 4.00 pm.
Materials Canvases and painting materials will be given by the school. The student should take care to bring an apron and cleaning materials as neutral soap.
Price 90 euros weekly (for the kids and adults course, an amount of 100 euros weekly will be charged for every pair of students e.g. father and son).
Registration fee: 80 euros.

Portfolio & Media Courses

Promote your work

Our program is specifically designed to guide the artist through the process of creating a professional portfolio and effectively leveraging social media platforms to showcase and sell the artwork. Throughout the programme, you will receive expert guidance from experienced artists and instructors. One of the key aspects of our program is the emphasis on creating a professional portfolio. We understand that a well-curated portfolio is essential for artists. Our instructors will assist you in selecting and arranging your best artworks, helping you create a cohesive and visually appealing portfolio that truly represents your artistic vision. In addition to portfolio development, our program places a strong focus on the power of social media platforms to promote and sell your artwork. Our instructors will teach you effective strategies to maximize your visibility on the media platforms.
